escrow, and every state change hits a hash-chained journal before it's

for now; an x402 bridge is the roadmap's next big step, which is why I'm
here. Live grid: https://api.ovriq.xyz/dashboard · Code:
https://github.com/BeMintalitet/ovriq — feedback on the escrow model very
welcome, especially from folks doing agentic payments.
